Leveraging Subject Matter Expert Speech-to-Text Models as a Novel Alpha Generation Strategy in Financial Markets

Abstract

The increasing sophistication of natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning technologies has opened new frontiers for information extraction and alpha generation in financial markets. This paper explores the potential of using speech-to-text models from subject matter experts (SMEs) as a unique source of investment insights and potential market alpha. By systematically analyzing expert discourse, investors can potentially uncover nuanced information not readily available through traditional financial reporting mechanisms.

1. Introduction

Financial markets have traditionally relied on structured data sources such as financial statements, press releases, and market reports. However, the advent of advanced NLP technologies presents an opportunity to extract valuable insights from less conventional information sources, particularly expert speech.

This study aims to investigate the potential of using speech-to-text models from subject matter experts as a novel approach to generating investment alpha, exploring both the methodological framework and potential implementation strategies.

Traditional financial theory suggests that superior investment returns (alpha) can be achieved by accessing and interpreting information more effectively than market participants. Speech-to-text models from SMEs represent a potential mechanism for reducing information asymmetry.

2. Methodology

2.1 Data Collection

Data collection involves gathering audio/video content from multiple sources:

  • Earnings calls
  • Industry conferences
  • Academic presentations
  • Expert interviews
  • Podcasts and webinars

2.2 Technical Framework

The technical approach includes:

  • Speech-to-text conversion using advanced models
  • Natural language processing techniques
  • Sentiment analysis
  • Contextual information extraction

3. Technical Components

3.1 Speech Recognition Models

Key technologies include:

  • Transformer-based models (Whisper, DeepSpeech)
  • Domain-specific fine-tuning
  • Multi-language support

3.2 Natural Language Processing

NLP techniques encompass:

  • Named entity recognition
  • Sentiment analysis
  • Topic modeling
  • Contextual understanding

4. Alpha Generation Mechanisms

4.1 Linguistic Signals

Potential alpha generation through:

  • Tone and sentiment variations
  • Subtle linguistic nuances
  • Comparative analysis across expert sources

4.2 Information Asymmetry

Advantages include:

  • Early detection of emerging trends
  • Implicit market signals
  • Contextual understanding beyond traditional metrics

Conclusion

This research demonstrates the promising potential of speech-to-text technologies in generating financial market insights. By leveraging advanced NLP and machine learning, investors can potentially uncover unique informational advantages.
